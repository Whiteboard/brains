Why we do it

Our generation will leave a mark on this world. As those before us we will be remembered for our failures and our accomplishments; what advancements we made, who we voted into leadership, and how we answered the call of others. This statement is not written as a punch line or an audacious pronouncement aimed at self-promotion. It is engrained in the fear that one day our impact will be analyzed.

We pay attention to the momentous occasions that have carried us through history; who was in charge, who carried the flag, and who lead us down the right path.

A Group of Brains was formed to do intentional work, to leave a mark greater than ourselves. As a band of friends we could think of no greater calling than this: to use our experience and talents to empower world changing ventures and organizations. What greater legacy than to help others realize their own?
