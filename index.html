Elders of the millenial generation,

we graduated into a society on the fray of recession and uncertainty. Against debt and restraint we became entrepreneurs, engineers, dreamers, and doers. We are A Group Of Brains, a consultancy stewarding tomorrow’s good by investing in and building outlets that cultivate leadership, novel ideas, and solutions.

We connect creative professionals and entrepreneurs to people and organizations who desire to reinvent how we do business and transform daily living. Our resources live, work, and play inside the pillars of vocational pathways, educational platforms, and emerging technologies.

We work for leaders.

We invest in education.

We build technology.

We were mandated to carry degrees, pieces of paper that symbolize financial security. For many of us this meant debt and society’s empty promises. Tired of meaningless banter – we are the generation whose inheritance is borrowed. Don’t stand or raise a fist in defiance, simply walk into your world and be yourself. Let the dreams within you flood the streets of our cities. There is no time, no moment—there is a here and now and that is all you need. We believe people are awesome and invest in those who believe the same. Simply stated, our work matters and our communities depend on it.

As technology continues to make knowledge accessible and invade all of us with infinite friend requests, we believe in space. The kind of space that cultivates friendships—the kind that propels society’s pioneers to share what they’ve learned. Rooted in history, we advocate for debate and common solutions. We build educational venues because there is always more to learn and sharing our experiences is a facet of life that makes being human so great.

At its core, technology wants to make our lives better. It simplifies actions, endorses connections, and expands possibilities. Heirs of the digital age, we were raised with computers in our homes and the internet at our fingertips; We know first-hand that the outcomes of technology are both constructive and destructive. We live in this strong and constant conflict, but we believe the destiny of tomorrow shouldn’t be left to chance. We believe the future is in our hands, and we boldly accept that responsibility. Our lives depend on it. Our children’s lives depend on it. Creating great technology is a moral obligation.

Why we do it

Our generation will leave a mark on this world. As those before us we will be remembered for our failures and our accomplishments; what advancements we made, who we voted into leadership, and how we answered the call of others. This statement is not written as a punch line or an audacious pronouncement aimed at self-promotion. It is engrained in the fear that one day our impact will be analyzed.

We pay attention to the momentous occasions that have carried us through history; who was in charge, who carried the flag, and who lead us down the right path.

A Group of Brains was formed to do intentional work, to leave a mark greater than ourselves. As a band of friends we could think of no greater calling than this: to use our experience and talents to empower world changing ventures and organizations. What greater legacy than to help others realize their own?
